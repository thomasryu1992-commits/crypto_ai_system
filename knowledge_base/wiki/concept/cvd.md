# CVD

## Definition
Cumulative Volume Delta tracks aggressive buyer versus seller activity.

## Bullish Interpretation
Rising spot CVD can confirm real demand behind price strength.

## Bearish Interpretation
Falling spot CVD during price rebound can imply weak demand or perp-led move.

## Risk
Exchange-specific CVD can diverge and should not be used alone.
