# Funding Rate

## Definition
Funding rate reflects the cost paid between long and short perpetual futures traders.

## Bullish Interpretation
Neutral or negative funding during price strength can imply under-positioned longs or trapped shorts.

## Bearish Interpretation
Excessively positive funding can imply crowded longs and liquidation risk.

## Risk
Funding must be compared against OI, basis, spot flow, and liquidation clusters.
