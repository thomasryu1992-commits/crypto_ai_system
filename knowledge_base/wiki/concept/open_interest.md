# Open Interest

## Definition
Open Interest represents the total value of outstanding derivative contracts that have not been settled.

## Bullish Interpretation
- Price up + OI up: trend continuation possible
- Price up + OI down: short covering possible

## Bearish Interpretation
- Price down + OI up: short buildup or trapped longs
- Price down + OI down: long liquidation or position unwind

## Risk
OI alone is not directional. Interpret it with price, funding, CVD, and liquidation levels.
